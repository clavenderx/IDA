ABOUT

[2025]

In November of every year at the Reykjavík Art Museum, Iceland, the Iceland Design Awards of the Iceland DesignMarch Festival takes place to award Icelandic designers and architects who have greatly contributed to the Icelandic design and architecture scene. The Iceland Design Award was created by Iceland Design, the Iceland Academy of the Arts, and the Museum of Design and Applied Arts. It has been supported by Business Iceland, SI - the Federation of Icelandic Industries, the Housing and Construction Authorities, Gróska and Science Park of the University of Iceland.
