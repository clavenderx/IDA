Sigríður Sigurjónsdóttir — Chairman of the Jury

Sigríður Sigurjónsdóttir is the Chairman of the jury and Director of the Museum of Design and Applied Art in Iceland. Her leadership has been instrumental in championing Icelandic design culture and elevating the profile of applied arts on an international stage.

Eva María Árnadóttir

Threading Icelandic Roots in Fashion

As Dean of the School of Architecture, Design and Fine Art at Iceland University of the Arts, she continues to influence the next generation of creatives while advocating for the growth and recognition of Icelandic fashion design.

"My journey in fashion began as a knitwear designer at Ice wear...

...where I developed a deep connection to Icelandic textiles and the craft behind them. Through this experience, I discovered how clothing is not only something we wear but, it can express identity, reflect culture, and carry the stories of the places that inspire us."

"I believe Icelandic fashion's originality comes from the challenges of being an island, far from manufacturing and production. These limitations have inspired us to reimagine constraints as opportunities for innovation."

"For me, fashion is not defined by a single style, but rather its an ongoing conservation between heritage and experimentation."

Most recently a part of the round table at the Icelandic Fashion Design Association in 2020 which recognizes designers for their work and contribution to Icelandic fashion design.

Bergur Finnbogason — Creative Director of EVE Online

Bergur Finnbogason is the Creative Director of EVE Online at CCP Games, one of the most celebrated and long-running massively multiplayer online games. His vision has shaped the unique aesthetic and player experience of EVE's universe for over a decade.

Sigurlína Margrét Osuala — Head of Ceramics

Sigurlína Margrét Osuala is the Head of Ceramics at the Reykjavik School of Visual Art. Her practice and pedagogy explore the intersection of material culture and contemporary craft, bringing international influence to Icelandic ceramic traditions.

Erling Jóhannesson — President of the Icelandic Artists' Union

Erling Jóhannesson is the President of the Icelandic Artists' Union, an organization representing artists across disciplines in Iceland. He has been a vocal advocate for cultural policy and the rights of creative professionals.

Guðrún Sóley Gestsdóttir — Journalist and Media Personality

Guðrún Sóley Gestsdóttir is a well-known Icelandic journalist and media personality with a long career covering culture, society, and the arts. Her public platform has helped bring design discourse to broader Icelandic audiences.

Tor Inge Hjelmdal — Architect and CEO at DOGA

Tor Inge Hjelmdal is an architect and the CEO of DOGA — Design and Architecture Norway. He leads one of Scandinavia's foremost institutions promoting design thinking and architectural excellence across the public and private sectors.
