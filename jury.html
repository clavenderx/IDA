Kristjana S. Williams - Visual Storyteller

Kristjana S. Williams is an globally recognized visual storyteller known for her unique collage-based style. Working across illustration, drawing, digital design and hand-cut collage, Williams has showcased work at the prestigious Victoria and Albert Museum.

Stefan Sagmeister — Graphic Designer

drag to rotate

Stefan Sagmeister is an influential contemporary designer and two-time Grammy winner whose work explores the impact design has on happiness and beauty. A frequent TED speaker, his exhibited globally, including The Happy Show, the most visited graphic design exhibition in history.

Dorte Mandrup - Architect

Dorte Mandrup is a Danish architect known for her environmentally rooted designs and structures that enagage with the landscape. Her notable projects include The Whale, Exile Museum and Kangiata Illorsua.

Hjalti Karlsson - Visual Communication

Hjalti Karlsson, co-founder of the New York design studio Karlssonwilker, is known for his distinctive approach to visual communication and branding. A recipient of the Torsten and Wanja Söderberg Prize, his work has shaped cultural organizations around the world.
